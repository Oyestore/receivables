## Introduction to Training

### Purpose of These Materials
These training materials are designed to help users of all roles effectively utilize the Milestone-Based Payment Workflow Module. Each section is tailored to specific roles, providing step-by-step guidance, best practices, and tips for maximizing productivity.

### How to Use These Materials
- Identify your role within the system.
- Navigate to the relevant training section for your role.
- Follow the modules sequentially or jump to specific topics as needed.
- Refer to the User Guide and Administrator Guide for more detailed information.

### Learning Objectives
Upon completing this training, users will be able to:
- Understand the core concepts of milestone-based payment workflows.
- Perform tasks relevant to their specific role efficiently.
- Utilize advanced features to enhance productivity.
- Troubleshoot common issues.

## Troubleshooting for Users

### Common Issues and Quick Fixes
- **Cannot log in**: Check username/password, caps lock, try password reset.
- **Milestone not appearing**: Check filters, search terms, or assigned projects.
- **Unable to upload evidence**: Check file size, format, and internet connection.
- **Notification overload**: Adjust notification preferences in your profile settings.
- **Page not loading correctly**: Try clearing browser cache or a different browser.

### Understanding Error Messages
- Common error messages and their meanings
- What information to note down if you need to report an issue

### When and How to Seek Help
- First steps: Consult this training, User Guide, or internal FAQs.
- Contacting your internal system administrator or support team.
- Providing necessary details for quick issue resolution (screenshots, error messages, steps to reproduce).
